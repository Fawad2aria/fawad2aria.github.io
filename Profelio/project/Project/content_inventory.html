Goal of the web site
This is a tour company, and the goal of the website is to make the information about the company and tours more accessible for people who are searching tours in Portland, OR.
Viewers
The tours are for visitors or residents who are interested in seeing and learning more about Portland.
Fundamental Analysis
The client addresses his concerns about looking professional and expert on the website. Furthermore, the website should be able to be viewed and visible in all screen sizes.
Demanding an action
The customer should be able to contact in case of any questions, get interested, be able to access all informations about the tours, and book a tour.
Competitive Analysis
Oregon Tour Co: This website looks very sample and basic with not having a lot of experience in the touring field. The good points are the easy ways to contact them, and the descriptional tour lists with the prices and pictures of the tour.
Evergreen Escapes: This website design is looking very professional and modern. Easy ways to contact them, discriptional tours, and the website is fittable in all different screens.
